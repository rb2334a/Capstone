What is it about?



This website is dedicated to the understanding and implementation of data points connected with the spread of COVID-19.


In the navigation bar, you will see two options apart from Home and the About Us/Contact Us page. The two pages mentioned are: Spread of COVID-19 and Vaccine Distribution. As a user, you have the choice to go into either of these two pages to explore the spread of COVID-19 across the United States, but also explore the distribution of vaccines across the United States.


Each page works in similar way. For instance, in the Spread of Covid-19 you only have to press on the dots located ontop of the map and explore the data provided. To find a new location inside that same state just simply move forward with the arrows located at the top right of the pop-up box.


The vaccine distribution map allows for a change in the legend! So feel free to play with the legend and explore the changes in spread and location.
